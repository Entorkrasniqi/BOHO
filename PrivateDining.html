Private Dining

ABOUT

A world apart, yet only a few steps away, here you can entertain in extravagant style. Our private dining room, Artemis, is the inner sanctum of Bacchanalia’s temple. Named after Bacchus’ secretive sister, Artemis offers escapism of the most sublime kind. A goddess of hidden wild places, she’s the ideal guardian of evenings spent feasting with a close circle of friends. Our Greco-Roman feasting menus offer lunch menus from 90£ per person and dinner from 120£ per person..
